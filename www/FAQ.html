We want to have a cat, but we already have a dog. Will they be able to live together?

Researches by Israeli scientists say that if a cat and a dog are up to 6 months old for a cat, and up to 1 year old for a dog and these animals are allowed to live on the same territory, then the chances that they will not conflict are very high. Therefore, if you already have a cat or a dog - pay attention to their age, and, add a neighbor to the recommended dates, since later it will be difficult to hope for peace.

What about pet health?

We guarantee you that all our pets are vaccinated and sterilized (except for puppies up to 5 months and those who are contraindicated for surgery under general anesthesia). Also in the shelter animals are examined and examined by a veterinarian. When you relocate, you enter into an agreement with a charitable foundation and receive a veterinary passport confirming the vaccination and all copies of the tests. We will also tell you everything we know about your pet's former life.
